👟 Adidas Sales & Margin Dashboard (Power BI)

Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 120M+ sales data, operating margins, and customer trends across regions and retailers.
🎯 Business Objective

To provide insights into:
	•	Sales performance across regions and retailers
	•	Monthly sales trends and seasonality
	•	Profitability and operating margins
	•	Customer segmentation (gender-based analysis)

🛠 Tools & Technologies
	•	Power BI (Data Visualization, DAX, Data Modeling)
	•	SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
	•	Excel (Data preprocessing)

🤖 AI Usage
	•	Used Claude and ChatGPT to:
	•	Generate optimized DAX measures
	•	Assist in SQL query writing
	•	Identify trends and insights from data
	•	Improve dashboard storytelling

📊 Key Features
	•	KPI Cards:
	•	Total Sales: 120M
	•	Units Sold: 2M
	•	Operating Margin: 4.08K
	•	Sales analysis by:
	•	Region (West, Northeast, etc.)
	•	Retailers (West Gear, Foot Locker, Amazon)
	•	Sales method (Online, Outlet, In-store)
	•	Monthly trend analysis for units sold
	•	Gender-based sales distribution
	•	Interactive filters for City and State

📈 Key Insights
	•	West region generates the highest revenue (~40M)
	•	West Gear and Foot Locker are top-performing retailers
	•	Online sales contribute the highest share (~41%)
	•	Sales show fluctuations with peaks around mid-year
	•	Male customers contribute slightly higher sales (~54%)
